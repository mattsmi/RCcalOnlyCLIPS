Easter Dating Method

In some countries of the Middle East, the Catholic Church permits Easter and its dependent movable feasts to be celebrated alongside her sister Orthodox Churches using the Julian calendar. Feasts of the sanctoral cycle — fixed feasts — continue to follow the Gregorian calendar.

iCalendar

iCalendar is a standard calendar format that enables users to create and share electronic calendars and scheduling information such as events, feast days, etc. The iCalendar standard is supported by most modern e-mail and calendaring software such as Microsoft Outlook, Apple iCal, Google Calendar, Evolution, and Mozilla Thunderbird — as well as the standard calendaring applications on Apple iPhones and Android smart phones and tablets. Providing the feasts in this format allows them to be added to calendars on a computer or smart phone — and thus to carry them about every day.

For these RC calendars, the calendar scale in the iCalendar file is always set to GREGORIAN, as the fixed feasts (or sanctoral cycle) always uses the Gregorian calendar — even if the option is chosen to display Easter and its dependent feasts according to the Julian calendar.
